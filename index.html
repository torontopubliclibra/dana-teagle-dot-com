The services I offer

I'm currently taking on freelance projects for graphic design, web and software development, digital content strategy, and website auditing. Please feel free to reach out and we can discuss your goals, deadline, and budget in more detail. I'd love to help make your dream project a reality!

There are a lot of variables when it comes to design and development work: platform & technology choices, functionality needs, custom design elements, hosting requirements, etc.

My standard process for new contracts usually involves laying out multiple options for clients up-front before I develop a unique road map for the project, and we work on finding a quote that compensates my estimated labour while meeting your budget and needs.

For smaller-scale projects, ongoing maintenance work, graphic design, and consulting, I generally work on an agreed-upon hourly rate.

I develop all of my web projects with consideration for responsiveness (functioning seamlessly across devices), search engine optimization, and web accessibility, and rigorously test for usability and performance before launch to ensure every potential user gets the ideal browsing experience.
